## Welcome back

Today, we're exploring Butler in more depth. Some of you may have loved the text. Some may have had trouble with it. So we're going to think more about this trouble.

## Writing exercise

Write for 5 minutes. The title of your piece is _The Trouble with Gender_

## Being in and Making Trouble

Let's discuss the following:

1.  What did you find troubling about Butler's text?
    
2.  What do you find troubling about either the notion of gender, or how gender is imagined / reimagined within your own cultural context?
    
3.  In her 1990 preface to her book, Butler writes, "I concluded that trouble is inevitable and the task, how best to make it, what best way to be in it." What do you think she means?
    
4.  What is the best way of making this trouble? What is the best way of being in it?
