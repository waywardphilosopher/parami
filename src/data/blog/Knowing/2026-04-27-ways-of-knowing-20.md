## Thinking about Your Assignment

Now, before we worry about the details, I'm going to get you thinking about your assignment with a writing exercise.

1. What reading have you most enjoyed (including the current one) from the second half of the course?
2. What ideas have you had that might be interesting to follow up on?
3. What puzzles or questions do you have about the readings that might be interesting to explore more?

## Breakout Groups

1. What might you want to explore?
2. What questions do you have about the assignment?

We'll then talk about this in the main room!
