Today, we're going to wrap up our work on the Zhuangzi. First, I want you to go into your breakout groups, and talk about the work you have done on the discussion board. What did you write? Why did you write it?

Then discuss the following:

*   What are the most interesting questions for you in this section of the text?
    
*   What puzzles are there about knowledge?
    
*   What new insights do you have?
    

To finish, we'll do a short writing exercise, thinking about the following:

*   What do you know about knowledge that you didn't know before you started reading the Zhuangzi?
    
*   What do you _not_ know that you thought you knew before?
    

## Assignment Briefing

We'll finish off the session with a briefing on the mid-term. We'll take some time to talk about this in groups, and discuss it together. The next class will be dedicated to the assignment.
